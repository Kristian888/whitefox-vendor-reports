# WHITEFOX Vendor Reports - Writing Style Guide

## 📝 BUYER FEEDBACK STYLE (Josh Phegan Methodology)

### Key Characteristics:
- **Direct quotes and specific language** - "Wouldn't pay over $1.95M"
- **Buyer positioning categories** - Strong/Moderate/Cold interest
- **Factual buyer profiles** - Professional couple, downsizer, investor
- **Specific objections and motivations** - Price concerns, timing issues, feature preferences
- **No sugarcoating** - Honest feedback about rejections and concerns

### Example Phrases:
- "Wouldn't pay over $X"
- "Won't commit until..."
- "Loves the location but concerned about..."
- "Strong interest, considering offer at..."
- "Ruled out due to..."
- "Very keen but needs to..."

### Structure:
1. **Strong Interest** - Buyers likely to make offers
2. **Moderate Interest** - Buyers needing nurturing/follow-up
3. **Ruled Out/Cold** - Buyers who have decided against

## 🎯 NEWS DELIVERY STYLE (Chris Voss Influence)

### Key Characteristics:
- **Tactical empathy** - Acknowledge vendor concerns upfront
- **Frame challenges as opportunities** - "This feedback gives us valuable positioning data"
- **Use calibrated questions** - "What would need to change for you to feel confident?"
- **Label emotions** - "It seems like the pricing feedback might be concerning..."
- **No/That's right moments** - Get vendor agreement before moving forward

### Example Phrases:
- "It seems like..." (labeling)
- "What I'm hearing is..."
- "The way I see it..."
- "Here's what this tells us..."
- "This actually gives us an advantage because..."

## 🎨 TONE AND LANGUAGE

### DO USE:
- Specific buyer quotes and feedback
- Concrete numbers and statistics  
- Honest assessment of challenges
- Strategic framing of feedback
- Professional but conversational tone

### AVOID:
- Generic "buyers love the property" statements
- Hiding negative feedback
- Overly sales-focused language
- Promises that can't be measured
- Industry jargon without explanation

## 📈 DATA INTEGRATION

When I process your PDFs, I will:

1. **Extract specific buyer quotes** for positioning section
2. **Analyze campaign metrics** for performance breakdown
